# Brand Overlay: PhotoShap

Technology. What it is: AI-powered photo booths, video booths and
interactive guest engagement systems.

Voice: slightly warmer and more guest-experience-focused than XSEDES
master, still engineering-led — the product is precise technology that
creates joyful moments. Audience: event agencies and planners choosing
guest-engagement tech, wedding and corporate event markets.

Known product family (describe capabilities, never invent specs or
pricing): Instagram Mirror, Retro, Vintage Mirror, Cycle Photo Booth,
Sketchy, Guest Audio Booth, black mirror booth. Kerala wedding lifestyle
is a recurring visual theme in brand imagery — reference it culturally
and respectfully (kasavu, nilavilakku, terracotta tones) as environment,
never as costume or cliché.

Asset naming, if ever referenced: photoshap-{product-slug}-{type}-{number}.webp
(PRD §32.6). All master XSEDES rules apply.
