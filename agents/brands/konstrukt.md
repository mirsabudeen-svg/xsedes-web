# Brand Overlay: KONSTRUKT

⚠ NAMING NOTE: approved brand docs say KONSTRUKT; a KONTRAKTOR variant
exists in some materials. If content is public-facing, flag the naming
question in your output notes rather than silently choosing.

A venture incubated inside XSEDES. Industry: Education Technology. What
it is: AI-powered learning platform for children focused on 3D printing,
robotics and digital manufacturing.

Voice: encouraging, clear, parent-and-educator friendly — but never
babyish and never hypey. Audience: parents, schools, makers-education
community in India.

EXTRA RULES (tightened, because the audience includes children):
absolutely no claims about learning outcomes without evidence; safety
first in any activity described; content must be suitable for parents
and educators reviewing on behalf of children. All master XSEDES rules
apply.
